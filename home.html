How it works

To find lost item

Step 1 : Click on Browse for lost item
Step 2 : You will see latest reported lost items in feed search if anyone reported your item
Step 3 : You can use search option on top of webpage which helps you to search with category and other filters

To report lost item if you can't find your item in feed

Step 1 : Click on Report lost item button
Step 2 : Fill all details it ask and submit
Step 3 : Wait until someone finds it and replay your post

To report found item which does not belogs to you

Step 1 : First search if anyone reported lost item which looks same as item you found in feed by clicking Browse for lost item and setting search filter lost
Step 2 : If no one reported then click on Report found item on home page
Step 3 : Fill all details it ask and submit and wait untill they contact you
